Explore The Tastes of Bruges

Belgian Sweets

Bruges Chocolate Shops

No matter what your sweet tooth craves, Bruges has something for everyone. With many renounded chocolate shops, waffes, bonbons, and other traditional Flemmish desserts, Bruges is a paradise for those who love sugar. Chocolate shops in Bruges are generally close together and walkable.

Flemmish Culture

Top Restaurants in Bruges

Traditionally, hearty foods such as meat, potatoes, and stews are common fare for Belegians. In Bruges, you will find an array of flavorful, filling traditional Flemmish restaurants, as well as new, modern takes on classics. Try your frites with mayo, you won't be disappointed!

Beer

Best Bars in Bruges

Beer, quite literally, is a Belgian way of life. You will find quaint breweries on almost every corner, offering local Trappist or Abbey beer and beers fro all over Belgium and Europe.
