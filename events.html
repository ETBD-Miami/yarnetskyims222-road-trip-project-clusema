Explore Bruges' Rich Culture Through Festivals

Bruges Beer Festival

Bruges Beer Festival

Every winter for the Bruges Beer Festival, taste a variety of beers, as well as enjoy concerts, games, and meet brewers who brewed the beer you drink! The Bruges Beer Festival features more than 280 beers from more than 70 breweries and eight top chefs cooking cuisine with beer. It is held below the ancient Belfry, seen in the movie In Bruges, which is located on the main market square in old historic Bruges and in a large heated tent on the Grote markt (main square). This is one of the few, if not only, festivals in the world where all seven Belgian & Dutch Trappist beers are available, including the elusive Westvleteren.

Bruges Chocolate Festival

Bruges Chocolate Festival

Every February join local bakers and chocolatiers in celebrating the famous craft of Belgian chocolate. At Choc'in Brugge, you can meet the best master craftsmen, visit the Chocolate Museum, enjoy themed menus in high-class restaurants, participate in workshops and tastings, sign up for a chocolate beauty treatment, or take a tour to discover the history of the love affair between the city of Bruges and everybody's favourite sweet.

Snow and Ice Sculpture Festival

Bruges Ice Sculpture Festival

Artists from all over the world gather to the Bruges Sation Square to showcase deatiled, beautiful sculptures carved from ice. An ice bar, ice skating rink, and Christmas shops are other things to do during the festival. The festival is located at Station Square in Bruges, directly in front of the main station building. It is just a 5-min walk from the city center. Every year, a whimsical theme based on pop culture is presented for the sculptors to base their works on.
